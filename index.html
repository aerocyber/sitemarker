    Why Sitemarker?

    storage

    Overwhelmed by Bookmarks?

    Browser bookmarks can quickly become a disorganized mess, making it hard to find what you need when you need it. Sitemarker helps you take control.

    cloud_off

    Privacy Concerns?

    Many online bookmark services require you to trust them with your data. Sitemarker is a local, private solution, giving you full control.

    search_off

    Hard to Search & Categorize?

    Generic bookmarking tools often lack robust tagging and search capabilities. Sitemarker provides powerful features for easy retrieval.

    Key Features

    bookmark_add

    Intuitive Management

    Easily add, edit, and delete your site bookmarks with a clean and user-friendly interface.

    sync_alt

    Import & Export

    Seamlessly import existing browser bookmarks or export your Sitemarker collection for backup or migration.

    style

    Customizable Themes

    Personalize your Sitemarker experience with a variety of built-in themes to suit your style.

    security

    Privacy-Focused

    Your data stays local and private, giving you full control without relying on cloud services.

    search

    Powerful Search

    Quickly find any bookmark with advanced search and filtering options, including tags and descriptions.

    restore

    Restore & Clear

    Accidentally deleted something? Sitemarker offers options to clear, permanently delete, or restore records.

    Why Choose Sitemarker?

    desktop_windows

    Native Desktop App

    Enjoy a fast, responsive, and integrated experience on your desktop, designed for efficiency.

    code

    Open Source & Free

    Sitemarker is free and open-source software, built with transparency and community contributions in mind.

    phone_android

    Android App Available

    Manage your bookmarks on the go with a dedicated Android application, keeping your sites handy.

    lock_open

    No Vendor Lock-in

    Your data remains yours. Sitemarker's open approach ensures you're never tied to a single platform or service.

    swap_horiz

    Your Data, Your .omio Files

    Full control over your data with easy export and import of records using the universal .omio file format.

    support

    Community Supported

    Benefit from an active community and dedicated developers who are continually improving the application.
